Taking the derivative of polynomials is just applying linearity and the power rule. An example

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\frac{d}{dx}\left(2x^3 + 3x^2 + x + 5\right) \\
&= 2\frac{d(x^3)}{dx} + 3\frac{d(x^2)}{dx} + \frac{d(x)}{dx} + \frac{d(5)}{dx} \\
&= 2(3x^2) + 3(2x) + 1 \\
&= 6x^2 + 6x + 1
\end{aligned}
$$
